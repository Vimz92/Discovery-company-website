COMPANY PROFILE

Founder and Managing Director: Mr. MoaffaK Ghaleb Sadek.

Established in the year 1992, DISCOVERY GROUP was founded under the leadership of "Mr. MoaffaK Ghaleb Sadek", who is the known to be the Founder and Managing Director of the Group. Starting off as a small trading firm, the company is moving from strength-to-strength, expanding its foothold in the region, and has established itself as the No. 1 building Materials Company along with other branches under its vast umbrella.

As one of the largest suppliers in the region, Discovery Group's business operations are dominated by Building Materials FZCO which brings a variety of state-of-the-art products available at showrooms strewn across UAE.
